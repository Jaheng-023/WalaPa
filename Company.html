Welcome to Otaku Threads!

At Otaku, we believe in the power of fashion to express individuality and confidence. Founded in 2024 by Geoffrey Estabalaya, Jerome Austriaco, Marinelle Facundo, Norriel De Guzman, and Hosanah Reign Nase, our journey began with a simple idea: to create clothing that empowers people to feel their best, every day.At Otaku Threads, we believe in fashion's ability to express individuality and confidence. The goal of our adventure, which was founded in 2024 by Geoffrey Estabalaya, Jerome Austriaco, Marinelle Facundo, Norriel De Guzman, and Hosanah Reign Nase, was to make clothes that allow people to feel their best every day.

Our Mission

Otaku Threads' mission is to bring you high-quality, stylish, and affordable clothing that enhances your unique style. We are committed to sustainability, ensuring that our practices are ethical and environmentally friendly. With a focus on exceptional customer service, we strive to create a shopping experience that is both enjoyable and fulfilling, meeting all your fashion needs while staying true to our core values.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use the finest materials to ensure our clothes are durable and comfortable.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly materials and practices to reduce our environmental impact.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your happiness is our top priority. We strive to provide excellent service and a seamless shopping experience.
